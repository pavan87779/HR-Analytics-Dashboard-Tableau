Client Requirements
The client (HR department of a mid-size organization) wanted a centralized visual dashboard to:

1.Track overall employee headcount, attrition count, and attrition rate.

2.Monitor active employees by department, age, gender, and education field.

3.Identify which departments, age groups, or job roles are facing the highest attrition.

4.Analyze job satisfaction ratings across job roles to prioritize employee engagement efforts.

5.Understand gender-wise attrition trends across age bands for targeted retention programs.
